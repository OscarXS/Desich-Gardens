About Us

Who We Are

"We are blessed with the best climate for the best and easy to maintain grass in Africa. We are the grass people!"

We supply, deliver and install freshly cut rolls of lawn. Our speciality is lawn but we also dabble in different forms of grass. This makes us quite versitile in the grass market and it always gives us the edge.

We also supply lawn dressing, compost and topsoil to both the commercial and domestic markets. We strive to be competitive in the gardening and horticulture industry and we will always offer the best price in the industry.

We employ a very high degree of professionalism and all our employees stand by our company rules and goals. We will always conduct ouselves appropriately while at your premises as we believe in beating and setting industry standards. We are the grass people and we will never disappoint you!

Mission Statement

We aim to provide a good quality product at a reasonable price, provide our customers with reliable service and trustworthy advice that enables them to live a healthier lifestyle. We aim to provide a range of varieties of premium quality turf so that we can meet the needs of our different customers. We are a goal orientated business that ultimately wants to provide families and businesses with turf that is of a superior quality, at an affordable price.

Our Values

We are a South African owned and run company; you deal directly with the owners who are also the growers of the lawn you are purchasing. We do the research on the turf so that you don’t have to.

Free Advice

Not sure which turf variety is best for you? Give us a call and let one of our friendly staff members help you choose the best instant lawn for you!

We Are Networked

Our network of freight companies consists mostly of trucks making it convenient to deliver your lawn with ease. Whether you need a little or a lot, we can deliver it to you on your desired day, Monday to Friday/Saturday (excluding Public Holidays). Deliveries can be arranged for any where in Gauteng and surrounding provinces.

24hr Delivery

We pride ourselves on prompt delivery and exceptional service, our rates are competitive and all our turf is delivered within 24 hours of harvest. Deliveries are made 5 to 6 days a week, to your door or if it is more convenient you can pick up your turf right from our main depot where there will be no delivery charge.
